One of the BALTIKs (applications build on **TRUST**) is called TrioCFD. It specializes in turbulence and multiphase flow modeling at the CFD scale. Click on its logo in order to check out its own documentation.

If you want to link it to your **TRUST** code, check out their [Quick Start](https://triocfd-documentation.readthedocs.io/en/latest/users/quickstart.html) and start experimenting with it through the following tutorial: [Turbulent Flow with Concentration](https://triocfd-documentation.readthedocs.io/en/latest/tutorials/turbulent_flow_concentration/index.html).
